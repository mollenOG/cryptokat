CryptoKatDAO Protocol

CryptoKatDAO is a governance and utility token used within the CryptoKat-Binance ecosystem to empower a decentralized community building at the forefront of web3.

→ CryptoKatDAO was launched as the base layer to foster developments within the CryptoKatDAO Ecosystem.

→ $CryptoKat is the ecosystem’s governance token, allowing CryptoKatDAO holders to participate in DAO.

→ CryptoKatDAO provides access to certain parts of the ecosystem that are otherwise unavailable, such as exclusive games, merch, events, and services.

→ CryptoKatDAO is a tool for third-party developers to participate in the ecosystem by incorporating $CryptoKat into services, games, and other projects.
